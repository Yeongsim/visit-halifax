Welcome to Halifax!

Halifax, Nova Scotia is a vibrant city filled with historic charm, scenic views, and unforgettable experiences. Here's a guide to some must-visit spots in the city.

Top Attractions

Citadel Hill

A historic fort offering stunning views and reenactments of Halifax’s military past.

Peggy’s Cove

A picturesque lighthouse and fishing village, perfect for photography and peaceful walks.

Halifax Waterfront

Enjoy shops, food, and scenic boardwalk views at this popular hangout spot.

Point Pleasant Park

Wander through trails and woods in this beautiful park right on the ocean’s edge.

Maritime Museum of the Atlantic

Explore shipwrecks, Titanic history, and maritime heritage in this fascinating museum.
